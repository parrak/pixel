# Copilot Demo Script

## Demo Flow

1. Manager opens the Streamlit dashboard and reviews urgent A/R, total work items, payer friction, and queue counts by role.
2. Biller opens the A/R Copilot for the 120+ day high-dollar hip claim and reviews the payer follow-up note and call script.
3. Denial specialist opens a prior-auth denial and reviews the denial summary, appeal packet summary, and appeal draft for reviewer validation.
4. Coder opens the Coding Copilot for the bundled modifier case and reviews the evidence-backed coding QA issue and missing-document checklist.
5. Workflow Assistant shows allowed actions, state, and audit trace expectations for the selected work item.
6. Manager reviews synthetic VCP-style metrics: dollars at risk, urgent items, owner-role distribution, and payer friction.
7. Payer Intelligence shows repeated denial categories, denial counts by payer and CPT, and upstream prevention themes.

## Featured Synthetic Scenarios

1. Clean paid orthopedic arthroscopy
2. Pain procedure prior-auth denial
3. Auth-approved CPT mismatch
4. Medical necessity denial with missing conservative therapy support
5. Modifier 59 bundled procedure risk
6. Implant supply charge capture miss
7. Underpaid commercial cataract claim
8. High-value 120+ day A/R follow-up case
